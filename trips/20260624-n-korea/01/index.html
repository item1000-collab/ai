🏛 Triumphal Arch (Arch of Triumph)

Located at the foot of Moran Hill, the arch was erected to commemorate the revolutionary struggle against Japanese colonial rule (1925–1945). It stands 60 meters high and 50 meters wide — larger than the Arc de Triomphe in Paris. The arch features 25,500 granite blocks, each representing a day of Kim Il Sung’s life up to his 70th birthday. A symbol of patriotism and national pride.

🔥 Juche Idea Monument

Located on the bank of the Taedong River, opened April 15, 1982. The monument is 170 meters tall (150m column + 20m torch). The three figures (worker with hammer, peasant with sickle, intellectual with brush) form the emblem of the Workers' Party of Korea. Take the elevator to the observation deck for a stunning panoramic view of Pyongyang.

Dinner at a restaurant in the city center. Transfer to the hotel. Check-in. Free time to explore the hotel surroundings (with your guide’s supervision).

🔍 Practical insights

🛬 Arrival & visa: Your visa is arranged by the tour operator. Keep your passport and arrival card safe. Photography is permitted in most public sites, but always follow the guide’s instructions.

💰 Currency: North Korean won (KPW) for local purchases, but euros, yuan or dollars are accepted in tourist shops and hotels. Bring small denominations.

📸 Photo tips: At the Juche Tower you’ll capture the entire Taedong River and the iconic Ryugyong Hotel. At the Triumphal Arch, try shooting from the lower stairs for dramatic perspective. Portrait photography of military personnel is prohibited.

🏨 Hotel: Typically a first-class hotel (e.g. Koryo Hotel or Yanggakdo International Hotel). Rooms include basic amenities, TV and private bathroom. International calls are limited — Wi-Fi is not widely available for tourists.

✈️ Flight note: Air Koryo flights are subject to schedule changes. Check with your group leader. The aircraft are modernized Soviet-built models (An-148, Tu-204) with in-flight service.

🎒 What to have with you

  • Light jacket or umbrella (June weather in Pyongyang is warm, but occasional rain)
