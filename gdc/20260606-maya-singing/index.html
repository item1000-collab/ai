The lesson emphasizes phrasing and vocal stretching: slowing down and stretching vowels to make singing feel natural — a go-to technique in American vocal coaching.

💪 Voice Fitness – vocal training as exercise

Pete (the second coach) brings up the term “voice fitness.” Maya expands on it: the vocal cords are muscles that need regular training, just like any other muscle in the body — a very practical, U.S. fitness-culture approach.

🎤 Andy's key advice (American coach style): “Slow down your voice … draw it out … then speed up again — that's where singing begins.”
→ An exercise for dynamics, breath control, and vocal agility, widely used in Broadway and LA studios.

Regular voice training improves tone, range, and pitch stability. “Voice fitness” blends the idea of a gym with a recording studio — a very American metaphor for vocal health.
