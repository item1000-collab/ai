📺 The era of the hangout context

Before smartphones & social media algorithms, ’90s youth culture revolved around physical proximity. Sitcoms reflected post‑Cold War optimism with deliberately low stakes: dating mishaps, career slumps, quirky roommate dynamics.

“Hangout comedy” — characters face romantic and nervous failures face‑to‑face, relying entirely on the people in the room.
