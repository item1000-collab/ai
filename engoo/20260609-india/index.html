🏔️ Thematic blocks: exploring India through landscapes & culture

🏔️ Northern grandeur

Darjeeling & Himalayas: High altitude, green tea plantations, Buddhist monasteries. Birthplace of Buddha in adjacent Nepal, enlightenment in India. Contrast between cool mountain air and deep valleys.

Key vocabulary: monastery, high altitude, tea gardens, pilgrimage, tranquil.

🏜️ Western deserts – Rajasthan

Land of Kings: Thar Desert, camels, majestic forts. Colour-coded cities (Pink City Jaipur, Blue City Jodhpur). Historic trade routes with the Middle East. “Raj” meaning ruling → fascinating etymology.

Conversational highlight: Anatoly compares Rajasthan to Rajshahi (Bangladesh) and explores royal heritage.

🎨 Southern traditions & festivals

Ganesha festival & temple architecture: Spirituality, vibrant celebrations, and ancient Dravidian style. Contrast between desert landscapes and tropical calm waters.

Cultural note: India’s festivals reflect unity in diversity – a core theme in the PDF presentation.

📖 Advanced vocabulary & idioms (from lesson context)

Land of contrasts – A phrase describing a place with extreme diversity (geography, culture, economy). India perfectly embodies this with snowy peaks and hot deserts existing side by side.
Nostalgia feelings – A sentimental longing or wistful affection for a period in the past. Anatoly mentions nostalgia for the sense of security during the USSR era.
Breathtaking landscapes – Scenery so beautiful or impressive that it takes your breath away. Used to describe Darjeeling’s mountains and tea gardens.
Exclusive opportunity – A unique chance that is not available to everyone. Anatoly describes visiting North Korea as an exclusive opportunity for Russian tourists.
To compile everything – To gather and organize information from various sources. Barkha praises Anatoly’s method of converting class recordings into new pages for memorizing vocabulary.
Transition of the economy – A fundamental change in economic structures (e.g., from USSR’s planned economy to market economy). Discussed regarding the 1990s transformation.
