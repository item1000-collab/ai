Migration · Environment & Power · Media · Global Nuclear Politics — Discuss, analyse, think critically.
📌 Short summary

Reading 1: US Defence Secretary Hegseth compares migration to Europe with an “invasion” during a D-Day speech. The Trump administration criticises European migration policies, while UK officials reject foreign interference.
Reading 2: A luxury resort project linked to Jared Kushner and Ivanka Trump in Albania faces protests. Environmentalists warn about the impact on a protected lagoon that is home to flamingos and sea turtles.
Reading 3: The official White House app lets users send preset messages to “text the president”. The default text reads “Greatest President Ever”, causing criticism and comparisons to authoritarian regimes.
Reading 4: North Korea rejects U.S. demands for denuclearization, labeling them an “anachronistic dream.” Kim Yo Jong announces continuous expansion of their nuclear arsenal ahead of Chinese President Xi Jinping’s diplomatic visit to Seoul.
