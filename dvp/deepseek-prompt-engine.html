🌿 Biodiversity & Indigenous Wisdom
The Amazon rainforest harbors an extraordinary richness of life and ancient cultural heritage. The following text reflects on its ecological importance and the threats it faces.

The biodiversity of the Amazon rainforest is unparalleled, containing a vast tapestry of life. Indigenous communities have thrived there for millennia, preserving ancient knowledge. However, modern deforestation poses a significant threat to this fragile ecosystem.
